Canada is one of the three countries located in North America. While being a self governing body inside of the British Empire, Canada was officially founded on July 1st 1867. Canada's primary cities include Vancouver, Ottawa, Montreal, Toronto, and Calgary. Vancouver is the largest western port and city. Ottawa is the country's capital city. Montreal is a major and extremely important industrial centre. Toronto is Canada’s largest financial hub as well as Canada’s largest city. Lastly, Calgary is the most populated city for Canada’s largest oil corporations. As of November Fourth 2021, Canada estimates that their country's exports rake in an average of 51 billion dollars per month. Canada’s top three primary exports are as follows, Crude Petroleum, Cars, and Gold. Crude Petroleum dominates Canada’s exports as raking in roughly $68 billion dollars. The second most valued export in Cars which generates roughly $41 billion. Lastly, gold comes in as roughly $15 billion for Canada. Canada’s culture is not as distinct from their neighboring country America. Canada’s culture has been described as a mix between the countries of British, French, and American culture. Canada is often described as being somewhat in line with America's culture, thus being a collective of other countries' cultural aspects combined to make a new unique culture.
